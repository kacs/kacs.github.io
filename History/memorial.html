In Memoriam


George R. Laure, the Chair of the Kalamazoo Local Section during its founding year, 1942, died on September 30, 2011, at the age of 99.

ACS member and former WMU Chemistry Professor Dean Cooke passed away on August 10, 2011. Dean represented KACS as Councilor for 30 years.

50-year ACS member Phil Bowman passed away on July 11, 2011.

28-year ACS member and WMU associate professor of chemistry John Chateauneuf passed away on June 17, 2011.

July 2010 - Memorial for Richard J. Margelis
