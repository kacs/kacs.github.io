KACS Annual Events

Executive committee meetings are usually held on the first Thursday of the month (except July). See the KalChemist newsletter last page (Upcoming events) for specific dates and locations of current year activities, meetings and special events.

January

New section Officers installed

Committees appointed

Past Committee and activities reports for Annual report completed
February

Annual Report Due

Applications for Project SEED Due

First ACS Tour Speaker (Feb, Mar or April)
March

Competitive Exam for High School students - initial round

Girl Scout Merit Badge (Feb or Mar)

Councilors to National ACS meeting

High School Science Symposium
April

Competitive Exam for High School Students, Final Exam

Section Awards nominations due

Chemists Celebrate Earth Day
May

Spring Awards Banquet
June

Project SEED Starts
July

No Executive Committee meeting
August

Project SEED Reception

Nominations for Section Officers

Councilors to National ACS meeting
September

Second ACS Tour Speaker (usually September or October)
October

Chemistry Spectacular put on by WMU Affiliate

Section Officer elections

National Chemistry Week - Chemistry Day at the Museum
November

Next Year Budget proposals due to Executive Committee
December

Annual Budget for following year approved by Executive Committee

Committee reports & activity reports for Annual report requested
