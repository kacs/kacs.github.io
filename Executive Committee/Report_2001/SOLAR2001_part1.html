 ****Important Notice Please Read****
 
The LSAC is pleased to announce the creation of a new EZ Annual Report Form. The EZ form was designed to ease annual report completion by reducing the reporting requirements for local sections. The EZ form consists of the Part I - Questionnaire, Part III - Financial Report and the Optional Part IV - Self-Nomination Form for ChemLuminary Awards. Sections that wish to submit the EZ form are not required to submit the Part II - Narrative and Appendices.All local sections are eligible to complete the EZ form UNLESS, they would like to self-nominate themselves for the LSAC Award for Outstanding Performance by a Local Section. Local sections may submit the EZ form and self-nominate themselves for any of the other awards lis ted in Part IV. Local Sections must postmark or submit their completed annual reports electronically by February 15, 2002 to be eligible for any of the awards
