Call for Abstracts

Presenters should email the following information to Schoffer@wmich.edu.

  • the poster title
  • author list with the presenter underlined and an asterisk(*) for the PI
  • institution information
  • an abstract to enter the prize drawing (150-200 words)

Undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged to participate. Your poster can either be newly prepared or a recycled poster. Poster presenters who submit their abstract (150-200 words) by 8 November can win a cash prize! Non-prize presentations will still be accepted until Friday 11 November to reserve an easel.
