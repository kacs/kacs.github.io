Discovery and Development of Simparica®

A Novel Companion Animal Isoxazoline Parasiticide

Dr. McTier is currently Therapeutic Area Head for Companion Animal Parasitology, and Research Director in Global Therapeutics Research, Veterinary Medicine Research and Development at Zoetis in Kalamazoo, MI. He will discuss the discovery and development of sarolaner (Simparica®) in Zoetis' labs here in Kalamazoo. This drug is a novel isoxazoline derivative and has unique structural features important for its potency and pharmacokinetic (PK) properties.

Undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged to participate. Your poster can either be newly prepared or a recycled poster. Presentations will be accepted until Friday 11 November.
