Fun in the Sun!

  • Free Food (Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian Options Provided)
  • Open to all Local Chemists and their Families
  • Family-friendly activities
  • Hope to see you there!

On Friday June 6, the Chemistry Graduate Student Association (CGSA) hosted a picnic at Oshtemo Park (7275 West Main) from 5-8pm. The purpose of this event was to introduce chemistry graduate and undergraduate students to the KACS community outside of the university. There were vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options provided. All members of the local chemistry community and their families, were welcomed at this event. Please come join us for a fun afternoon at the park on June 6th!

Report from the 2014 WMU Chemistry Graduate Student Association (CGSA) Picnic

By Sarut Jianrattanasawat

On June 6, the CGSA hosted its 4th annual cookout for local chemists at the Oshtemo Township Park. First and foremost, CGSA would like to thank KACS for the generous financial support. The event was successful. We had more than 40 attendees, including faculty, staff, undergraduate and graduate students from WMU, and KACS members from around Kalamazoo, as a well as some chemists-to-be. Many participated in activities such as tennis and soccer. We are glad to see this event gaining popularity as a local tradition.
