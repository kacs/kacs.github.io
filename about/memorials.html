KACS Memorials

  • James Howell passed away on Saturday 16 November 2013 at the age of 81. His legacy as a chemist and teacher will be remembered fondly.
  • KACS member for over 55 years and outstanding chemist Jackson Boling Hester Jr. pased away on 2013-10-10 at the age of 80.
  • George R. Laure, the chair of the Kalamazoo Local Section during its founding year, 1942, 2011-09-30 at the age of 99
  • ACS member and former WMU Chemistry Professor Dean Cooke passed away on 2011-08-10. Dean represented KACS as Councilor for 30 years.
  • 50-year ACS member Phil Bowman passed away on 2011-07-11.
  • 28-year ACS member and WMU associate professor of chemistry John Chateauneuf passed away on 2011-06-17.
  • July 2010 - Memorial for Richard J. Margelis
