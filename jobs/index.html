R&T Chemist - Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients

Albemarle

At Albemarle, we've built a thriving international business by envisioning a limitless future and putting innovation to work to improve people's lives. That's our culture. That's our commitment. And when you join our team, it's an opportunity you share with talented men and women around the globe. Anyone can give you a job. At Albemarle, we believe you deserve more. Something big enough to build a future. Big enough to change the world.

Job Description

Lead and participate in area and project activities in support of departmental goals, including development and implementation of methods for new processes. Independently develop/validate innovative methods for new processes. Participate on project teams as both member and/or leader and effectively coordinate all related project activities, including resolution of customer and technical issues. With minimal supervision, assist in preparation of regulatory documents. Train junior staff.

  • Develop and validate methods for new production processes in support of the drug development process.
  • Identify innovative methods/improvements applicable to the product development process.
  • Participate on project activities, including coordination of other staff, communicating timeframes, and ensuring completion of individual assignments.
  • Prepare regulatory documents, technical reports, development protocols, and other required documentation.
  • Serve as technical resource for the department, and as required to meet corporate goals.
  • May provide supervision, guidance, and direction to lower-level technical/scientific staff.
  • Effectively interpret and defend analytical data and reports.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.
  • Manage hazardous waste in compliance with regulations

All employees have the responsibility and authority to raise health, safety and environmental concerns related but not limited to training, operating procedures, batch records, general plant conditions and engineering standards. He/she may bring concerns and suggestions to their Direct Supervisor, Area Manager, SHE Manager, or Site Leadership Team Member who will determine the appropriate corrective action up to and including shutting down the process until the issue is safely resolved.

Requirements

  • Five years relevant experience OR
  • AA in a related scientific field with four years relevant experience OR
  • BS/BA in a related scientific field with three years relevant experience OR
  • MS in a related scientific field with one year relevant experience
  • Demonstrated ability to perform advanced analytical procedures.
  • Experience in validation of applicable methods and knowledge of related regulatory requirements.
  • Demonstrated leadership and ability to work effectively with others.
  • Knowledge of regulatory requirements and pharmaceutical, analytical and/or manufacturing processes.
  • Ability to prepare/present oral and written presentations

Education

Associates: Chemistry (Required)

Are you…

The Chem-Able matching grant program sponsored by the Kalamazoo Section of the American Chemical Society might help. The purpose of a Chem-Able grant is to assist chemical professionals and students in obtaining training or experience to improve chances of employment or re-employment.
