Part-time Instructor – Biochemistry

Western Michigan University

The Department of Chemistry at Western Michigan University is currently seeking to hire a Part-time Instructor in the area of Biochemistry. The duration of the appointment will be May 11, 2015 – July 1, 2015. The candidate is expected to teach Introduction to Biochemistry and supervise two laboratory sections. The students comprise sophomores, juniors and seniors at WMU. Candidates are required to have a PhD in Biochemistry. Interested applicants should send a curriculum vitae and a cover letter to Dr. Gary Blackmer, Chair of the Department of Chemistry at gary.blackmer@wmich.edu.

Chem-Able Matching Grant Program

Are you…

  • an unemployed or underemployed chemical professional?
  • trying to enter the chemistry workforce?

The Chem-Able matching grant program sponsored by the Kalamazoo Section of the American Chemical Society might help. The purpose of a Chem-Able grant is to assist chemical professionals and students in obtaining training or experience to improve chances of employment or re-employment.
