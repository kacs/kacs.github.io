The Kalamazoo Section of the American Chemical Society held a Science Café event on 18 May 2010.  It featured Dr. Todd Barkman, a biologist at Western Michigan University, who led a discussion of “Biochemical Diversity: From odorants to stimulants.”  Dr. Barkman’s cross-disciplinary expertise was evident as he clearly and humorously described the chemistry and the biology associated with biosynthesis of methylsalicylate (“Oil of Wintergreen”) and caffeine.  About 30 people participated in the presentation and discussion.  Science Café was held upstairs at Martini's Pizza in Kalamazoo.

The next Science Café is planned for September.
