Abstract Submission Open for Bell's Poster Session

Undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged to participate. Your poster can either be newly prepared or a "recycled" poster. Poster presenters who submit their abstract (150-200 words) by Monday, Nov. 4 can win a $50 cash prize! Non-prize eligible presentations will still be accepted until Friday, Nov. 8. Visit our event page for more information.

ACS Project SEED

Project SEED provides summer research experiences in chemistry for high school students from low income households. Each Project SEED fellowship costs our local section about $2000. Our goal is to raise enough funds each year to fund at least two projects per summer.

You may donate directly to our KACS Project SEED fund on PayPal. Kalamazoo ACS is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
