Climate Change Workshop

Saturday 25 January 2014

The event is free but registration is required .

In collaboration with the KACS, several faculty experts from the WMU Climate Change Working Group will provide training on global climate change in a free half-day workshop on January 25th. They will lead hands-on computer-based exercises on four major aspects of climate change:

  • The Scientific Basis of Climate Change
  • Effects of Climate Change on Ecosystem and Human Health
  • Climate Change Mitigation: The Roles of Personal Behaviors and Alternate Energy Technologies
  • Social Responses to Climate Change: Economic and Political Considerations

Great turnout at 2013 Chemistry & Culture: Elements of Dance
Friday 1 November 2013 at the KIA.

KACS became a ChemLuminary Award finalist for the "Chemistry and Culture: Elements of Dance" event (9 November 2012) at the Kalamazoo Institute of Arts in the category of "Most Innovative Activity or Program". Out of 186 Local Sections nationwide, we are thrilled to have been recognized in this category and thank all the donors and volunteers who helped us make this event a success (179 attendees).
