Project SEED 2010 Reception

A reception for our Section's two Project SEED participants during the Summer months of 2010 was held in the Chemistry Building Lobby area on Monday, August 9, 2010.  The students, their mentors, family members and KACS members gathered to encourage Basza Miller (KPS - Loy Norrix and KAMSC) and Sebastian Brainerd (KPS - Kalamazoo Central) and to recognize the support given by their mentors, Dr. Gellert Mezei and Dr. Ekk Sinn, respectively.  This reception brought to a close our Local Section's 19th year of participation in Project SEED.
